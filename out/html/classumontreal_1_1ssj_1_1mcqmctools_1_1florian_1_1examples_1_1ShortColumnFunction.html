Detailed Description

This class implements the so-called short column function \(f(Y,M,P)\) as explained in https://www.sfu.ca/~ssurjano/shortcol.html.

More precisely,

\[ f(Y,M,P) = 1 - \frac{4M}{bh^2Y}-\frac{P^2}{b^2h^2Y^2}, \]

where \(b\) and \(h\) are the deterministic width and depth of the cross section (in mm), respectively. The random variable \(Y\) is assumed to be lognormally distributed while \(M\) and \(P\) are normally distributed with covariance Matrix \(\Sigma\). \(Y\) is independent of \(M\) and \(P\).
