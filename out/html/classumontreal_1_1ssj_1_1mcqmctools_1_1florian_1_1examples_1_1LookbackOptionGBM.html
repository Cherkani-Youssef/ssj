Detailed Description

Implements the current value of a Lookback call option under a geometric Brownian motion.

This value is defined as the maximum of the (discounted)values of the asset at several observation times. Here, the observation times are automatically selected as equidistant. The Brownian motion is constructed via bridge sampling.
